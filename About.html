Who am I?

Hello, I'm Brandon Tulsie. Born on September 18th in 1998 and at the current age of 19.
As a person I am umempathetic although I try to be as empathetic as I possibly can at times, but emotions are hard, this can lead to a lot of social awkwardness, so for the most part I act on how I think is best and not what I feel is best. I try my best to provide an unbiased and honest opinion on any matter. I put the needs of others before my own as I personally enjoy helping others.
I don't have a particularly strong pressence as a person and as such I do my best to try and "blend in" so to say.
Officially I have zero work experience so make of that as you will.

My Hobbies

I enjoy reading good books, many people find books boring for some odd reason. I have spent most of my life on video games, I recall a ridiculous comment from somone once "You have a retard amount of hours on this game (Overwatch)", which I still think is hilarious, I have an interest in drawing which I have been slowly picking up on again (I'm still terrible), I was also top 1% in Destiny with a playtime of 4175 hours(174 days). I'm also developing a growing interest in creative writing. Music from all genres is also something that I consume for hours on end, instrumental music is simply magnificent.
